About Us:

AMC Engineering College was established under the Paramahamsa Foundation Trust and located in Bannerghatta National park road, Bangalore approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education), Govt., of India and affiliated to VTU (Visvesvaraya Technological University). Also, courses are accredited by NBA.

At AMC Engineering College, our UG and PG programs provide a solid foundation for technical training, employment and future growth. The institute has spread out in 52 acres of land with unique style of infrastructure for each department endorsed by well equipped laboratories, equipments and machines.

AMC Engineering College Library is a resource center for teaching, learning & research. Being the heart of the college, it is home for all the information services. It plays a proactive role in enabling access to information resources of all kind and providing innovative, responsive and effective services to meet the changing needs of the students and faculties. Also, the library is considered to be a state of art digital library, E-Learning Center. This Digital Library provides the knowledge base for the students.

All Departments Available: Computer Science Engineering Civil Engineering Electronics and Communication Engineering Electronics and Electrical Engineering Information Science and Engineering Mechanical Engineering Aeronautical Engineering AI and ML Engineering
